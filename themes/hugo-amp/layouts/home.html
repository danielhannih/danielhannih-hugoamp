PsyCat Games

{{- partial "svg/psycat-story-line.html" -}}

Why making games?

What fascinates me about the gaming industry is that it requires so many different skills to work together. The whole process behind designing, building, marketing and monetizing is astonishing. As a passionate gamer myself and with entry-friendly software to enable making games, it was a no-brainer to try it out.

How it envolved.

With a handful of games in place, I wanted a website so people could find them. The site should also support articles around entertainment. In the end the website was designed mobile first and built on top of Google’s AMP with Hugo as a static site generator. This way the site nearly instantly loads if coming from Google Search.
